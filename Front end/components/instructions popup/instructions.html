Instructions

Itsy Bitsy Boom is a game where you uncover squares on a grid, trying to avoid spiders.
Click a square to start; if it's a spider, you lose. If it's not, it reveals a number indicating nearby spiders. Use these numbers to deduce where spiders are and mark them with flags to avoid clicking on them. Win by uncovering all safe squares; lose by clicking on a spider.It's a game of logic and luck, so enjoy and try to beat your best time!
